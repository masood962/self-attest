# ✍️ Self Attest — Free PDF Self Attestation Tool

A free, browser-based tool to self-attest PDF documents online. No uploads to server, everything works locally in your browser.

🔗 **Live App:** [Click here to use](https://yourusername.github.io/self-attest)

---

## 🚀 Features

- 📄 Upload any PDF (single or multi-page)
- ✍️ Upload your signature (PDF or image)
- 🎨 **Auto background removal** — only ink is kept, white background removed
- 🖊️ **Ink color changer** — Black, Blue, Red, Green or any custom color
- 📍 **6 position options** — Top/Bottom × Left/Center/Right
- 📏 Adjustable sign size and margin
- ⚡ Instant download — no data sent to any server
- 🔒 100% private & secure

---

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. Open the app
2. Upload your **Document PDF** (the file you want to self-attest)
3. Upload your **Signature file** (PDF or JPG/PNG with your signature on white background)
4. Adjust background removal, ink color, position, size
5. Click **"Self Attest Document"**
6. Download your attested PDF instantly!

---

## 📌 Use Cases

- Self attestation of marksheets (10th, 12th)
- Degree certificates
- Caste certificates
- Identity documents
- Government job applications
- Any document requiring self attestation

---

## 🔒 Privacy

All processing happens **100% in your browser**. No files are uploaded to any server. Your documents stay completely private.

## 💻 Tech Stack

- HTML5 + CSS3 + Vanilla JavaScript

- [PDF-lib](https://pdf-lib.js.org/) — PDF editing
